Extraction Points

Those are portal-like gateways that you can use to exit the game without losing your snook/NFT. They appear randomly and dont last long so keep an eye out and act fast.

Resurrection

Snook is an unforgiving game. If your super
rare snook suddenly dies, not all is lost. You

to its awesomeness and rarity).
